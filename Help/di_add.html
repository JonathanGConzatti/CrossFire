Adding Cards in DeckIt!

There are many ways to add cards with DeckIt!.

  1. Push the Add button.
  2. Double click the card.
  3. Press <Ctrl-A>.
  4. Press <RightArrow>.
  5. Click the middle mouse button (on three button mouse.)
  6. Drag & Drop
  7. Select 'Add' from the right-click pop-up menu.
