Removing Cards in DeckIt!

There are many ways to remove cards with DeckIt!.

  1. Push the Remove button.
  2. Press <Ctrl-R>.
  3. Press <LeftArrow>.
  4. Click the middle mouse button (on three button mouse.)
  5. Drag & Drop
  6. Select 'Remove' from the right-click pop-up menu.
You may remove one card or an entire type of card.
