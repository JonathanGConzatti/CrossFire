Card Warehouse Introduction

The CrossFire Card Warehouse is used to keep an inventory of your Spellfire collection.

The inventory made with this can be exported to David Maxey's Spellfire Database 2.x. Importing from Spellfire Database 2.x is also available.

There are four main areas of Card Warehouse:

  1. The Menu Bar

  2. Card Selection List

    The card selection list contains all of the cards of the selected card set. The card set can be changed from the menu bar or by pressing the comma (,) or period (.) key.

    You can search for a card with the search box below the selection box. Simply enter the word (case insensitive) to search for. To repeat the search, press or <PageUp> or <PageDn> to search backwards or forwards, respectively.

    You may use the keyboard to "navigate" through the selection list. The <Home>, <End>, <Up>, <Down>, <PageUp>, and <PageDn> keys work as expected. Pressing the <RightArrow> key will increase the "On Hand" quantity and the <LeftArrow> will decrease the "On Hand" quantity.

    Many cards can be changed at one time. Pressing <Control-A> will select all cards. <Control-Left-click> will toggle a card's selection and <Shift-Left-click> will select all cards from the last card clicked to the current. All selected cards will be changed when the "Premium Card" status, "Max Wanted", or "On Hand" is changed.

  3. Report Buttons

    Inventory
    Generates an inventory report of the current card set in the selected format and display method.

    Want List
    Generates a report of the wanted cards of the current card set in the selected format and display method.

    Extras List
    Generates a report of the extra cards of the current card set in the selected format and display method.

  4. Card Information and Quantities

    Premium Card
    Denotes that the card is above ordinary. (The Bell of Might is a good example.) The reports will mark these cards with an asterick (*) or in bold face.
    Wanted
    Changes the maximum wanted quantity of the selected card.
    To change the quantity:
    • Type in the quantity and press <Return>.
    • Push the "-" or "+" buttons.
    • Right-click the card and select Increase or Decrease Wanted.
    • Use the Change Wanted Quantity utility.

    On Hand
    Changes the quantity of the selected card you have.
    To change the quantity:
    • Type in the quantity and press <Return>.
    • Push the "-" or "+" buttons.
    • Press the <LeftArrow> or <RightArrow> keys.
    • Right-click the card and select Increase or Decrease On Hand.
